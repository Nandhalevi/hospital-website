Laser Dentistry

Home Laser dentistry

At Saraswat Dental Hospital, we offer the latest Laser dentistry for painless and quick recovery.

Laser dentistry uses lasers to treat dental conditions. Laser dentistry is one of the most comfortable treatment options for most problems involving hard and soft tissue. It is very patient-friendly as there are no drills or other non-laser tools used. You will have a relaxed treatment with absolutely no room for anxiety.

What is Laser Dentistry

LASER stands for “light amplification by the stimulated emission of radiation.” The laser instrument creates a focussed beam of narrow light. When this light touches the area to be treated, the tissue reacts to it and this allows correction, shaping and disinfection of the area.

Saraswat Dental Hospital offers laser dentistry for:

  • Teeth whitening
  • Depigmentation of gums
  • Root canal treatment
  • Mouth ulcer treatment

Benefits of using laser dentistry

  • Minimum or no sutures with soft tissue lasers
  • Minimum bleeding as lasers help clotting of blood
  • Minimum tissue damage
  • Minimum chance of infection as lasers sterilise the area treated
  • Faster completion of Root Canal Treatment
  • Quicker healing of cold sores, mouth ulcers
  • Faster healing
  • Anaesthesia may not be necessary for some procedures

Lasers can make dental treatments more efficient, cost effective, and comfortable. For the best Laser Dentistry Service in Jaipur, consult the experts and Saraswat Dental Hospital.

Frequently asked questions?

What types of lasers are used?

Hard tissue lasers are used to treat teeth. Soft tissue lasers are used to treat gums and mouth ulcers.

What are the hard tissue procedures performed?

Hard tissue lasers are used to treat teeth. Soft tissue lasers are used to treat gums and mouth ulcers.

  • Tooth preparation and dental fillings without anaesthesia and traditional drills
  • Destroying bacteria to prevent infection and promote long-term tooth health
  • Treating tooth sensitivity by sealing tubules to prevent sensitivity to heat and cold
How are lasers used to treat gums and tissues?

Soft tissue lasers are used to:

  • Treat “gummy smile” by reshaping gum tissue
  • Lengthen crown by reshaping both gum tissue and bone for healthier tooth structure
  • Remove soft tissue folds to help fit dentures better and painlessly
  • Treat cold sores quickly and reduce pain
  • Whiten teeth
How much does laser dentistry cost?

The cost of laser dentistry can vary depending on the procedure and the type of laser used, but laser treatments require fewer sessions than traditional dental treatments and so the total cost for the entire treatment may be less expensive.
