Orthodontics

Home Orthodontics

Straighten and aligns your teeth for a perfect smile and bite.

If your misaligned teeth are making it difficult to bite, chew or talk, we have the solution- a range of aligners that offer the best way to make your teeth align perfectly.

We use aligners to correct

  • Gaps in your teeth
  • Gaps in your teeth
  • Open bite (when teeth do not meet in the middle when you bite)
  • Cross bite (when teeth don’t align when you close your mouth)

Clear aligners (invisible braces)

The Perfect Way to Teeth Straightening….......

These are the clear transparent custom-made tray made up of special material which straighten the teeth.

How To Do Aligners

Our team of dentists will do the following procedures to schedule your clear aligners treatment plan.

  • Initial assessment including medical and dental history
  • 3D scan of upper and lower teeth or impression of teeth
  • Photograph of face and mouth (Intraoral and Extraoral photograph)
  • Lateral Cephalogram or OPG Radiograph

After assessing all the above listed procedures, your aligners will be fabricated.

The aligners are fabricated by automatic machine and a set of aligners (10 to 40 sets) will be given to you. Their design will fit them snugly on your teeth and this will help move your teeth to the desired position for best smile and best result.

When to Go for Aligners

  • Spacing in between the teeth
  • Mild crowding of teeth
  • Deep overbites
  • Open bite
  • Severely rotated teeth
  • Case with multiple teeth missing
  • Cross bite

Metal braces

Small brackets (metal fixtures) fixed on the teeth with a thin metal (archwire) wire running through them secured with elastic ties or metal bands. The archwire is adjusted gradually to bring teeth into alignment.

Ceramic braces

Ceramic braces use ceramic brackets instead of metal brackets. They also have archwire secured to brackets with elastic ties or metal bands. Better cosmetic appeal as ceramic is similar to teeth in appearance.

Fast braces or self-ligating braces

Self-ligating braces have an in-built mechanism in the metal brackets to hold the archwire in place. It’s easier to clean and causes less discomfort as there are no elastic ties or metal wires.

Advantages of fast braces

  • Invisible aligners
  • Treatment completed in very short time
  • Very comfortable
  • Easy to remove while eating and cleaning
  • Fewer dental visits
  • • Better dental health
