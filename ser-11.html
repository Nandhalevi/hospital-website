4 Step Sterilisation

At DR. Neeraj Saraswat Dental Hospital, we take sterilisation very seriously. We follow the most stringent sterilisation protocols to ensure complete safety for you, our patient. Our 4 Step Sterilisation practice offers complete protection by ensuring 100% sterilisation of all the instruments and equipment, including drilling hand pieces and drill bits. This process removes all viruses and spores completely.

The disinfected instruments are sealed in sterile pouches, which are opened only when needed and in front of you, so you rest assured that you are completely safe when you visit Saraswat Dental Hospital for treatment.

The 4 Step Sterilisation Process

Step – 1/h4>

Instruments are immersed in disinfectant solution for a minimum of 30 minutes. They are then scrubbed and cleaned in running water and cleansing solution.

Step – 2

The cleaned instruments are then cleansed in an Ultrasonic Cleaner Chamber. They are then dried and packed in sealed pouches.

Step – 3

They are then sterilised in ‘B-Class’ Autoclave for 100% sterilisation.

Step – 4

The sterilised packages are stored in UV Light storage cabinets in the sterilisation room maintaining their sterility till needed.
