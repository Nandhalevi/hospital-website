Dental implants are like artificial tooth roots made of strong materials such as titanium of zirconium that are placed in your jawbone. Over time, these implants fuse with your bone, creating a sturdy foundation for replacement teeth.

Our experienced team will carefully perform the dental implant procedure to ensure it is done right. Once the implant has fused with your jawbone, we will attach a custom-made crown that looks just like your natural teeth. This will give you a complete set of teeth that looks great and works well.

Even if you don't have enough bone in your jaw, don't worry! Our dentist can place the implant above your jawbone or inside your cheekbone to still give you a permanent solution.

We offer different types of dental implants to suit different needs:

Single Tooth Implant:

This is used when you have one missing tooth. We will place an implant and attach a replacement tooth that blends in with your other teeth.

All-on-Four Implant:

If you need to replace all the teeth in one arch (either upper or lower jaw), we can use four implants to support a full set of replacement teeth. It will feel secure and help you chew comfortably.

Full Mouth Implant:

When you need to replace all your teeth in both the upper and lower jaw, our full mouth implant option is perfect. We will carefully place multiple implants to support a full set of replacement teeth.

72-Hour Implant:

We understand that time is important to you. With our 72-hour implant option, we can complete the entire process, including placing the implants and attaching the crowns, in just 72 hours. This means you can have a new smile in no time!

Our dental hospital is proud to offer immediate implant solutions, allowing you to enjoy a restored smile quickly. Whether you need an instant fix or prefer to do it gradually, our friendly dental team is here to help you achieve a healthy and beautiful smile.
