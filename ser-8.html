Periodontics

Home Periodontics

Protect gums. Preserve teeth.

Periodontics deals with the soft tissues that surround and anchor your teeth to the jawbone. Our periodontist treats all conditions of the gums, ligaments that attach teeth to the jawbone, the protective hard layer called cementum that covers the roots and the jawbone that has the sockets that hold the teeth.

Gum health is crucial for healthy teeth and we ensure it. Preventive care, treatments for all conditions and management of chronic conditions are best provided at our dental hospital.

Latest treatments, advanced surgical procedures and complete guidance is provided by experts.

Flap surgery (for gum disease)

  • Gum surgery to cure infection
  • The gum is opened, root cleaned and bone reshaped if necessary
  • The gum is then sutured back

Ginvectomy

  • Procedure done to remove excess gum tissue that covers teeth
  • The gum is reshaped for better aesthetics and smile

Scaling and polishing (cleaning of teeth and polishing)

  • Scaling removes plaque and tartar to prevent gum disease and tooth decay
  • Polishing smooths and polishes teeth after scaling

Bone graft, soft tissue graft, guided tissue regeneration for receding gums

  • Regenerates gum tissue lost due to various causes
  • Helps preserve teeth

Mouth ulcer and canker sore

  • Medical treatment for persistent sores
  • Medicines to relieve symptoms
