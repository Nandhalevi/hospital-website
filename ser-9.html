Oral Surgery

Home Oral Surgery

When you need oral surgery, we provide the best.

Some problems of the teeth, jaw, and surrounding oral and facial tissues and structures may require surgical correction. These surgeries are called oral surgeries.

Oral surgeries include tooth extraction, implantation of artificial teeth, bone grafts in the jaw gum grafts and surgery to correct deformities either congenital or caused due to trauma.

We have the expertise to handle all oral surgeries and perform procedures to correct all types of oral problems from widespread tooth decay to oral cancers.

Latest technology is used to deliver the most advanced oral surgeries for best results. Complete assessment of your problem and your overall health condition helps our expert determine the treatment option best suited for you. As we provide customized oral surgeries and excellent support and care, the recovery will be smooth and complication free.

Minor surgery

  • Extraction of tooth
  • Surgical extraction of wisdom tooth
  • Surgical extraction of impacted tooth
  • Oral cancer surgery

Major surgery

  • Cyst enucleation – removal of cyst from jaw of oral cavity
  • Jaw tumour surgeries
  • Jaw fracture surgeries like maxilla, mandible
  • TMJ joint fracture (jaw fracture)
